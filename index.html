How to Play TypeRacer
1
Choose Your Difficulty

Select your preferred difficulty level from the dropdown menu:

  • Easy Simple words and short sentences
  • Medium More complex vocabulary and longer sentences
  • Hard Advanced vocabulary and technical terms
2
Read the Sample Text

A random text sample will appear in the gray box. Take a moment to read through it before you start typing.

3
Start Typing

Click in the text input field and start typing the sample text exactly as shown. The test will automatically begin when you type your first character.

Tip: Focus on accuracy over speed - mistakes will lower your overall score!
4
Monitor Your Progress

Watch your real-time statistics update as you type:

  • WPM: Words Per Minute
  • Accuracy: Percentage of correct characters
  • Time: Elapsed time
  • Progress: Completion percentage
5
Finish the Test

Complete the test by either:

  • Typing the entire text (automatic completion)
  • Pressing Enter to finish early
  • Clicking the Stop button
Goal: Try to achieve high WPM while maintaining 95%+ accuracy!
Use the Retry button to get new text samples
